                            Windows and Office activation related
.
Here you can modify the SKU of Windows and Office and activate them.
Some instructions require relevant files to be prepared in advance.
.
Please select the features you need:
.
    [1] Activate the Embedded version of Windows with KMS
    [2] Convert Office RT to Office Pro Plus VL
        (relevant files need to be prepared by yourself)
    [3] Activate Office Pro Plus VL with KMS
    [4] Export tokens and DigitalProductId under Windows RT Build 9200
    [5] Import the tokens folder of a custom source
        (It is recommended to do this in PE 
         when the RT8.0 beta image is just released) (Chinese only)
    [6] Convert Windows RT 8.1 to Industry Embedded
        (relevant files need to be prepared by yourself)
    [7] After converting Windows, 
        modify the group policy to enable sideloading 
        and associate Appx files for double-click installation
    [8] Uninstall the original Office product key to 
        resolve the problem that hinders activation
    [0] Exit
.
Enter the number corresponding to your selection and press Enter:
.
